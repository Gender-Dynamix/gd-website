1. Information We Collect

We may collect information such as your name, gender identity, and email address when you voluntarily provide it for referrals or contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only for the purpose for which it was provided – for communication or referrals.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with third parties unless required for website functionality (e.g., hosting providers, form processing services like Formspree). We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

4. Cookies

Essential Cookies Only

Gender Dynamix Aotearoa uses strictly necessary cookies to ensure our website functions correctly. These help with:

  • Security: Preventing CSRF attacks on our referral forms
  • Performance: Ensuring the site loads quickly for our users
  • Preferences: Remembering if you've seen our privacy banner and age verification status

You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, this may affect certain functionality of our website.

Under the NZ Privacy Act 2020, we do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Your data remains your own.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Links to Other Websites

Our website contains links to other websites, including external resources for gender-affirming products. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Some linked websites may contain age-restricted content.

7. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Any changes to the privacy policy will be reflected on this page. Check this page regularly for updates.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us at referrals@gdnz.org

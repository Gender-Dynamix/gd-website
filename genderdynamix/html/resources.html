Gender Dynamix Aotearoa uses strictly necessary cookies to ensure our website functions correctly. These help with:

Transcend Garments

Transcend is a small New Zealand-based business, dedicated to creating high-quality gender-affirming garments. Each piece is handcrafted in small batches to ensure quality and consistency. Transcend is passionate about addressing gaps in resources for transgender individuals and actively seeks input from the community to develop new, accessible products tailored to their needs. As a brand, we aim to push the boundaries of what gender-affirming clothing can be.

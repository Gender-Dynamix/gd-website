Cookies & Privacy Information

Essential Cookies Only

Gender Dynamix Aotearoa uses strictly necessary cookies to ensure our website functions correctly. These help with:

  • Security: Preventing CSRF attacks on our referral forms
  • Performance: Ensuring the site loads quickly for our users.
  • Preferences: Remembering if you've seen this banner before.

Under the NZ Privacy Act 2020, we do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Your data remains your own.

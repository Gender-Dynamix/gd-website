    Your Impact Matters

    The support we provide makes a real difference in the lives of trans and nonbinary people in our community. Many face high levels of discrimination, poverty, homelessness, and mental health challenges.

    Your generous donation helps real people in our community thrive by being their authentic selves.

    Mental Health Support

    Individual and group counseling for trans and gender diverse people.

    Peer Support

    Individual or group Peer to peer support led by trained facilitators with lived experience.

    Education & Workshops

    Community education and training for businesses, schools, and healthcare providers

    Whānau Support

    Resources and guidance for families and support networks
